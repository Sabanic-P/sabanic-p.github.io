About me

I am a PhD student at the Technical University of Munich (Systems Research Group), supervised by Prof. Pramod Bhatotia. In my research I focus on security and confidentiality with a focus on cloud systems. To be more precise, I focus on how to make cloud systems trustworthy and confidential, with the help of technologies such as Confidential Virtual Machines, Libraries OSes, and confidential accelerators.

I graduated at TUM with a Master's degree in 2023, after completing my Bachelor's in 2020. For more information, please see my CV.
